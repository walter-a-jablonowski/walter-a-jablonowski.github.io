AI is not a classic program: it can behave unexpectedly and make mistakes that aren’t immediately obvious. Without monitoring and verification, that remains a risk.

With the right protection mechanisms, end-to-end observability and measurable evaluation, a black box becomes a system that you and your customers can trust.

What does observability mean?

You can see at any time what the AI is doing and why – logging and monitoring make decisions traceable.

How is quality measured?

Through defined test cases and metrics. This lets you recognize improvements and deteriorations (regression) objectively.

What about data protection?

Operation either locally or on your own server, with support for a GDPR-compliant implementation.

Also for existing AI systems?

Yes. Security, observability and evaluation can also be added retroactively.

Data protection from the start: operation either locally or on your own server – with support for a GDPR-compliant implementation.
