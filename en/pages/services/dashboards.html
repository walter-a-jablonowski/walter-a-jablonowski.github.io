Important information often lives in many systems: databases, ERP/CRM, Excel spreadsheets, emails. A custom dashboard brings it together – without you having to replace your existing systems.

The built-in AI works directly with the connected data: via sidebar or voice it prepares analyses on request or triggers actions. And because the dashboard is modular, it simply grows with you. With over 25 years of database experience, I focus on reliable numbers.

What does a dashboard cost?

Small dashboards are available at a fixed price, larger ones are billed by effort. In a free initial consultation we assess your case concretely.

Which systems can be connected?

Databases (e.g. MySQL, SQL Server, SQLite), ERP and CRM systems, Excel spreadsheets, emails and APIs – individually or combined.

Where does the dashboard run?

On request locally or on your own server – your data stays under your control. I support a GDPR-compliant implementation.

How current is the data?

Depending on the source, updated live or at fixed intervals. On request with alerts as soon as values exceed or fall below defined limits.

What can the AI in the dashboard do?

You ask in natural language – via sidebar or voice. The AI prepares data (e.g. an analysis by region) or triggers actions, such as a reminder to a customer.

Can we extend it later?

Yes. Dashboards are built modularly – new tiles, data sources and AI features can be added at any time.

Why work with me?

A dashboard is only as good as the data behind it. That is exactly my strength: decades of experience with databases, combined with the latest AI.
