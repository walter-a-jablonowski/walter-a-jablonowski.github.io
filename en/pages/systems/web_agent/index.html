Website AI Agent

An AI assistant your visitors can talk to.

Answers questions in real time, guides visitors to the right page and speaks the visitor’s language – right on your website.

Try it now Contact

You’re using it right now – the button is bottom-right.

What is it?

A small assistant that runs in the bottom-right corner on every page. Visitors click the microphone and ask their question by voice. The assistant knows your website, answers instantly – and can guide them to the right page.

  • Speaks instead of typing – natural real-time voice
  • Knows your offering – tuned to your content
  • Actively guides through the website
  • Available on every page and remembers the conversation

Features

Speak instead of type

Real-time voice dialogue with a natural voice and low latency.

Knows your website

Tuned to your content and offering – answers real questions.

Guides to the right page

Suggests relevant pages or navigates straight there.

Multilingual

Replies automatically in the visitor’s language (DE/EN …).

Always there

Persistent overlay on every page; continues the conversation after a page change.

How it works

  1. Visitor clicks the microphone and speaks
  2. The assistant understands and answers instantly with voice
  3. On request, it guides them to the right page
  4. How: Browser → your Worker (key & context secure) → AI voice model

Sensitive data – the API key and the context about your offering – stays server-side (e.g. a Cloudflare Worker), not in the visitor’s browser.

Optionally, a live transcript can be shown so visitors can read along with what was said and answered.

Customizable

  • Persona, greeting and example questions are freely configurable
  • Navigation mode: actively guide or just offer a link
  • Live transcript on/off, optionally as a runtime switch
  • Launcher style: round microphone button or text pill („Ask AI“)
  • Languages extensible, AI model interchangeable

Privacy & control: The API key and context stay server-side, not in the client code. No tracking cookies needed, navigation is limited to your site („sandboxed“) – with support for a GDPR-compliant integration.

Frequently asked questions

What does it cost?

Setup from €1,000 · plus ongoing usage costs (AI model/Worker). The price depends on scope and customization – the initial consultation is free.

Which languages are supported?

German and English are included out of the box, and more languages are possible. The assistant replies automatically in the language of the page.

Does it run on my website?

Yes. The assistant is added as a small script; the key and context run through your own Worker or server.

Do visitors need a microphone?

For the voice dialogue, yes. The greeting and hints also work without one – and the microphone is only used after a click and permission.

What about privacy?

No tracking cookies are needed, sensitive data stays server-side, and navigation is limited to your site. I support a GDPR-compliant integration.

Which AI is behind it?

Currently Gemini Live. The model can be swapped without changing the integration.
