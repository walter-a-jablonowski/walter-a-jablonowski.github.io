What is it?

Chat with Data is a chat interface for your company data. You type a question in everyday language, the AI translates it into a safe database query behind the scenes, runs it and explains the result – including a fitting chart. No SQL, no report tools, no waiting on IT.

  • Questions in natural language instead of complex queries
  • Answers as a table, a plain-text summary and a chart
  • Knows your data model – tailored to your database
  • Runs on your server or fully local – your data stays with you

Features

How it works

  1. You ask your question in natural language
  2. The AI reads the schema and builds a validated query
  3. The result appears as a table, text and chart
  4. How: Chat → AI agent (tool-calling) → validation → your database

The AI agent retrieves the database schema on its own, picks the right tables and validates every query before it runs. That keeps results reliable and the database protected.

The language model is interchangeable: cloud (Google Gemini, OpenRouter) or local (Ollama) – depending on how sensitive your data is.

Tailor-made for you

Chat with Data is not a rigid off-the-shelf product but the blueprint for an individual AI data solution. I adapt it to your data sources, your vocabulary and your workflows:

  • Connect your databases (MySQL, SQL Server, SQLite …) – individually or combined
  • Additional sources like files, PDFs or emails can be integrated on request

Frequently asked questions

What does it cost?

Small projects at a fixed price, larger ones by effort. The price depends on data sources and customization – the initial consultation is free.

Does it work with my database?

Usually yes. MySQL, SQL Server and SQLite are included out of the box, and further sources – including files, PDFs or emails – can be connected.

Where does my data run?

On your own server or fully local. With a local model (Ollama), neither data nor questions leave your premises. I support a GDPR-compliant implementation.

Can the AI change my data?

No. Only read-only queries are allowed, and every query is validated against the schema beforehand – write access is technically ruled out.

Which AI is behind it?

Google Gemini, OpenRouter or a local model (Ollama), as you prefer. The model can be swapped without rebuilding the solution.

How quickly is it ready to use?

We start small with a clearly scoped data source and expand step by step – so you see first results quickly.
